Faqs

Your questions answered

What happens if a housekeeper calls out?

This is inevitable and can be one of the most stressful aspects of housekeeping. We built our team in preparation for this. If someone calls out or quits last minute, we have trained staff on backup.

How will I know if there is a problem at my property?

If we find any problems you will be notified right away. We take notes and pictures of everything - good and bad - and upload those into your property account online. We also understand the claims process with the most common booking platforms, and will assist if you need to file a claim.

Do you take care of restocking?

We do! Some hosts prefer to purchase items themselves and some hosts prefer us to do it. Whichever you prefer, we will take inventory during each turnover to ensure your property is properly stocked at all times.

Do you co-host?

We are not co-hosting at this time, but we will put you in touch with experienced, successful co-hosts that you will love.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, contact us today!

What's the best way to reach us?

You can call or text us at (479) 252-9881. You can also email is at cozycabinclean@gmail.com. We'll respond promptly!
